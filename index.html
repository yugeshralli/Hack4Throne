About the College

Rajalakshmi Engineering College is one of the leading ISO-9001-2000 certified; NAAC and NBA accredited engineering colleges established under self-financing scheme in 1997. The college has been growing from strength to strength in the last 20 years and moving ahead to become a Centre of Excellence in Engineering, Management Education, Research and Development.

The college is located in a serene atmosphere amidst many multinational companies in Thandalam, about 25 kms from Chennai on the Bangalore National Highway (NH4), providing the right industrial ambience for the budding engineers. The institution currently offers 11 UG programs, 9 PG programs, and 9 Research programs.

About the Department

The Department of Information Technology was established in the year 1998, offers one UG programme (B.Tech - IT) and one PG programme (M.E.Software Engineering). It has been recognized as Research centre by Anna University for collaborative research programme which leads to M.S. (by research) and Ph.D. B.Tech (IT) programme was accredited for 3 years by the National Board of Accreditation in the year 2015. The department is active in funded research and consultancy works
