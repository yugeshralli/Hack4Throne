About the College

Rajalakshmi Engineering College is one of the leading ISO-9001-2000 certified; NAAC and NBA accredited engineering colleges established under self-financing scheme in 1997. The college has been growing from strength to strength in the last 20 years and moving ahead to become a Centre of Excellence in Engineering, Management Education, Research and Development.

The college is located in a serene atmosphere amidst many multinational companies in Thandalam, about 25 kms from Chennai on the Bangalore National Highway (NH4), providing the right industrial ambience for the budding engineers. The institution currently offers 11 UG programs, 9 PG programs, and 9 Research programs.

About the Department

The Department of Information Technology was established in the year 1998, offers one UG programme (B.Tech - IT) and one PG programme (M.E.Software Engineering). It has been recognized as Research centre by Anna University for collaborative research programme which leads to M.S. (by research) and Ph.D. B.Tech (IT) programme was accredited for 3 years by the National Board of Accreditation in the year 2015.

The department has received the funding of rupees 1.2 crores from various funding agencies like DST, AICTE, UGC,CSI since 2014. The department has two special labs namely Assistive Technology Lab and Big Data Analytics Lab.

Rules and Conditions

Eligibility:

  1. The 'Hack4Throne' is open to teams with minimum of three or maximum of four excluding mentor , student members from the same college irrespective of department.
  2. Students must be 18 years or older to participate in the Hack4Throne contest.
  3. There should be a faculty member from the same college identified and authorized by the institution for supporting the students as their Mentor.

Prizes:

  1. Cash prize worth Rs.50000/- will be awarded to winners.
  2. No prize alternative will be offered.
  3. Prizes are not transferrable. Only members registered in the team at the beginning of the event will be eligible for the prizes.

Registration:

  1. All team members should register as a team and pay a non-refundable registration fee of Rs.250/- per head using the payment link on the web site.
  2. The mentor is exempted from the registration fee but has to register with the team.
  3. Every team should have a team name without which the registration process is Incomplete.
  4. There is no limit on the number of teams from the same college. But all teams should have an authorized mentor identified.
  5. Each team should have a team leader and mentor as primary points of contact for communication.
  6. All queries and correspondence with Hack4Throne 2017 Program Team shall be carried out by the Team Leader and/or Mentor.

Submission:

  1. Teams shall submit their entries to 'Hack4Throne' in prescribed format and only through official link provided. Submissions through channels other than prescribed, will not be accepted.
  2. Each team can submit only one idea as their formal entry to the 'Hack4Throne' contest.
  3. Incomplete entries, corrupt files and associated missing/wrong details will disqualify the submission without any further communication.
